-----------------------------------------------
ABOUT THE MOD:
-----------------------------------------------
Turn minetest's boring 2D torches into super amazing awesome 3D ones!

Based on 3d_torch mod for Minetest, by Infinitum and Semmett9
https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?f=11&t=8352

All 3D models were created using Rubenwardy's NodeBoxEditor
https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?id=2840
